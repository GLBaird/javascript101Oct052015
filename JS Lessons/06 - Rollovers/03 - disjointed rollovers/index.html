Disjointed Rollovers - Mondial House, London

We are now looking at disjointed rollovers, where a link can trigger a change in an image which is not containted within the tag. Here we have a selection of images of Mondial House in London.

Mondial House was built by BT in the late 1970s and took 5 years to complete. It was designed as a gigantic supercomputer using white plastic, and was full of machines and telephony equipment for linking the world as BT's flagship international telephone exchange. Sadly it was demolished in 2006 to make way for a bland block of offices made from chrome and glass.
